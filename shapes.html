Summary

Pokemon Shape is used in the Pokedex to categorize pokemon by their body style.

Source: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net

Averaging base stats by shape will show us if their are any significant differences in the base stats of differently-shaped Pokemon.

Base Stats

Pokemon Base Stats

Base stats are one of the underlying values that help determine the growth rate for each of a Pok√©mon's six major stats:

HP, Defense, Attack, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

They are a good indicator of the potential battle ability of the Pokemon.

Overall Base Stats

The categories with more upper extremeties (arms, tentacles, wings) have much higher base stats than categories that are defined by lower extremities (legs, quadruped).

Individual Base Stat Observations

  • You would expect that the armor class would have high Defense and Special Defense, however it is below average in both stats.
  • Head Category Pokemon have exceptionally high Defense.

Best Shape Category

If you sum all the base stat averages by category, Tentacle has the highest total.

We can assume that most lower teir Pokemon belong to the fish, blob, ball, armor, and legs categories.
