Summary

Egg Groups are categories which determine which Pokemon are able to interbreed. They are based on biological traits of Pokemon Species. They generally allow Pokemon with similar biologies to breed.

Source: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net

Averaging base stats by egg group will allow us to see what similar species outperform others.

Base Stats

Pokemon Base Stats

Base stats are one of the underlying values that help determine the growth rate for each of a Pok√©mon's six major stats:

HP, Defense, Attack, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

They are a good indicator of the potential battle ability of the Pokemon.

Overall Base Stats

While most egg groups are balanced, others have abonormally high stats or abnormally low stats.

Individual Base Stat Observations

  • There is only one pokemon in the 'ditto' category: Ditto. It is the only pokemon that can breed with any pokemon, including Legendaries.
  • The 'no-egg' group has abnormally high stats because this is the group that all Legendary Pokemon are in.
  • Monster and Mineral Pokemon have extremely high defense but lower speed.

Best Egg Category

If you sum all the base stat averages by category, 'no-eggs' has the highest total.

The 'no-eggs' group is assigned to powerful Pokemon to prevent players from breeding and obtaining more of them in a given game.

Because of this, it makes sense that they would have an overwhelmingly higher total.
