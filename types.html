Summary

Types are properties for Pokémon and their moves. As of Generation VI, there are 18 types. A Pokémon may have either one or two types: For instance, Charmander is a Fire type, while Bulbasaur is both a Grass type and a Poison type.

Source: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net

It's important to note that for this analysis, if a Pokemon has two types, I will only be using its first type.

Averaging base stats by types can tell us which types are generally stronger than others.

Base Stats

Pokemon Base Stats

Base stats are one of the underlying values that help determine the growth rate for each of a Pokémon's six major stats:

HP, Defense, Attack, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

They are a good indicator of the potential battle ability of the Pokemon.

Overall Base Stats

Overall, the base stat distribution among Pokemon types seems to be well-balanced.

Individual Base Stat Observations

  • Rock type Pokemon have exceptionally high attack and defense but lack speed and special attack. Steel type Pokemon have similar attack and defense but do not suffer the same consequences.
  • Bug type Pokemon have noticably lower stats than other types. This could be due to the fact that bug pokemon, on average, evolve earlier than any other type.

Best Type Category

If you sum all the base stat averages by category, Dragon has the highest total followed closely by Steel.
