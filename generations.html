Summary

A generation is a grouping of the Pokémon games that separates them based on the Pokémon they include. In each generation, a new set of Pokémon, moves, and Abilities that did not exist in the previous generation are released.

Source: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net

To date, there are eight generations. However since the newest generation was released very recently (Nov 19), I won't be including it.

Averaging base stats by generations will show us if newer generation Pokemon are stronger than older generation Pokemon.

Base Stats

Pokemon Base Stats

Base stats are one of the underlying values that help determine the growth rate for each of a Pokémon's six major stats:

HP, Defense, Attack, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

They are a good indicator of the potential battle ability of the Pokemon.

Overall Base Stats

Starting at Generation IV, the base stats of Pokemon appear to have increased significantly.

Individual Base Stat Observations

  • While the other stat values appear to be increasing with the newer generations, Generation VI's Attack stat decreased. However, in the next generation, the Attack stat increased to its all time high of 84.
  • The Speed and HP stats have been relatively the same throughout the generations.

Best Generation Category

If you sum all the base stat averages by category, Generation VII has the highest total.

We can expect the next generation of Pokemon to have higher base stats, or at least higher base stats than generations I-III
