Pokemon Color Group Analysis

Heat Map of Base Stats by Pokemon Color Group.

Base Stats Total by Pokemon Color Group

Summary

Each Pokemon is assigned a color grouping in the Pokedex.

While the actual colors of the Pokemon can vary, the color listed in the Pokedex is usually the color most apparent or covering each Pokemon's body.

We can use this to group the Pokemon by their color and observe the average base stats by color group.

Base Stats

Pokemon Base Stats

Base stats are one of the underlying values that help determine the growth rate for each of a Pok√©mon's six major stats:

HP, Defense, Attack, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

They are a good indicator of the potential battle ability of the Pokemon.

Overall Base Stats

The heatmap shows that the overal stats by color group appear to be balanced.

If a color group is high in one stat, it will have one with a lower average to balance it out.

Individual Base Stat Observations

  • Gray Pokemon seem to have very high Attack and Defense base stats but are lower in other stats.

  • White Pokemon have a higher base stat average.
  • Brown Pokemon have a lower base stat average.
  • Pink Pokemon have an abnormally low speed stat.

Best Color Category

If you sum all the base stat averages by category, White has the highest total.

This could be due to a higher perportion of Legendary Pokemon in the category.

Legendary Pokemon are rare and not usually allowed in tournament play due to their overwhelming power.
