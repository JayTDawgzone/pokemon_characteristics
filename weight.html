Summary

Each pokemon is assigned a color grouping in the Pokedex.

We can use this to group the pokemon by their color and observe the average base stats by color group.

Overall Base Stats

The heatmap shows that the overal stats by color group appear to be pretty well balanced.

Gray-colored pokemon seem to have very high Attack and Defense base stats but are lower in other stats

White pokemon have a higher base stat average.

Brown pokemon have a lower base stat average.

Best Pokemon in Each Category

The best pokemon in each color category appear to be mainly legendary pokemon.
