Summary

The weight and height of Pokemon are stored in the Pokedex because certain moves have a weight modifier depending on the Pokemon that uses it.

Source: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net

It would make sense that if the Pokemon was very heavy, their speed would be relatively slow.

Does the weight or height of the Pokemon affect their speed?

Weight vs. Speed

The weight of the Pokemon doesn't appear to have any affect on the Speed stat of the Pokemon.

Even if the Pokmon is exceptionally heavy, its Speed stat won't be affected all that much.

Furthermore, Pokemon that are very light seem to be slower than the heaviest Pokemon.

There is no correlation between the weight of the Pokemon and its Speed stat.

Height vs. Speed

Similar to the Pokemon's weight, there is no correlation between the height of the Pokemon and its Speed stat.
